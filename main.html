Our Family, Our Work

Classic Woodworks in Oxford, MI is a family owned custom woodturning, woodworking and cabinet shop. We specialize in wood turning and one of our favorites is large diameter turned pedestal table bases. We can also duplicate your vintage turnings such as spindles, newel posts, porch posts, etc. We look forward to hearing from you as to how we can assist you with your home decor or historical restoration project.
